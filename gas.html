General Academic Strand

What is the GAS strand?

The GAS (General Academic Strand) is an academic track in senior high school
that provides a well-rounded and flexible curriculum. It is suitable for various fields before choosing a college course.


What career paths can I pursue after completing the GAS strand?

Graduates of the GAS strand have the flexibility to pursue various college as liberal arts, communication, social sciences, education, and other disciplines
that require a broad academic background.


Is the GAS strand suitable for students who are undecided about their future careers?

Yes, the GAS strand is specifically designed for students who are still
exploring their interests and have not yet decided on a specific career path.
It allows them the flexibility to sample a variety of subjects before making
more focused academic and career decisions.
