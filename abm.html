Accountancy, Business and Management

What is ABM?

The ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management) strand is an academic track in
senior high school that focuses on subjects related to business, finance,
entrepreneurship, and management. It aims to prepare students for careers in
the fields of accountancy, business administration, marketing, finance, and related areas.


What career paths can I pursue after completing the ABM strand?

ABM graduates can pursue careers in accounting, business administration, marketing,
finance, human resources, entrepreneurship, and related fields. The strand provides
a strong foundation for those interested in pursuing roles in the business and financial sectors.


Is the ABM strand suitable for students interested in starting their own business?

Yes, the ABM strand is suitable for students interested in entrepreneurship.
The curriculum includes subjects that provide insights into business planning,
financial management, and the principles of entrepreneurship, preparing students
for starting and managing their own businesses.
